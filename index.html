About Us

Charity: Water was founded in 2006 with a clear mission: to bring clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries.

We believe in 100% transparency. That means every penny you donate goes directly to fund water projects. Our team works closely with local partners and organizations globally, empowering communities with sustainable solutions.

To date, with the help of private funders, we've funded over 180,000 water projects in 29 countries, benefiting more than 20 million people.
